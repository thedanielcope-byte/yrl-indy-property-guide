A full-service MIBOR brokerage serving Central Indiana buyers, sellers, investors, and agents — led by Daniel Cope.

Meet Daniel Cope, REALTOR

At the heart of Your Realty Link is Daniel Cope, a licensed Indiana real estate broker with years of experience helping Central Indiana residents buy, sell, and invest in real estate. As a REALTOR with deep local roots, Daniel has built a reputation for straight talk, market knowledge, and putting clients first — every single time.

Daniel's story is a Central Indiana story. He knows the neighborhoods, the school districts, the commute corridors, and the communities that make this region special. Whether a client is buying their first home in Greenwood or selling a longtime family property in Carmel, Daniel brings the same level of care and expertise to every transaction.

Daniel can be reached directly at 317-201-6323 or csirealtyteam@yourrealtylink.com.

Our Brokerage Story

Your Realty Link was built on a simple idea: real estate should feel less like a transaction and more like a partnership. We're not a corporate franchise with a call center. We're a locally owned, brokerage based in Indianapolis at 2302 E Southport Rd — and we're proud of it.

The brokerage serves the full Indianapolis metro and beyond, covering communities from Zionsville and Carmel in the north to Greenwood and Bargersville in the south, from Plainfield and Avon in the west to McCordsville and New Palestine in the east. If it's Central Indiana, Your Realty Link knows it.

Our agents are agents with access to the full MLS, which means clients get comprehensive market data, real-time listing information, and the kind of negotiating leverage that only comes with being connected to the market at the highest level.

Buyers: From first-time buyers navigating pre-approval to seasoned buyers looking for a move-up home or investment property, YRL represents buyers at every stage. Investors: Indianapolis is consistently ranked among the top real estate investment markets in the country, and Your Realty Link has the market knowledge to help investors find the right properties — whether that's a single-family rental, a fix-and-flip, or a multi-family asset.
