📍 SW Decatur County🏫 South Decatur Schools🏡 $150s – $280s+~70 min to Downtown Indy

Westport sits in southwestern Decatur County along State Road 3, just south of Greensburg and within reasonable driving distance of Indianapolis, Columbus, and Cincinnati. With a town population of about 1,400 and a school district that draws from a broader rural area, Westport offers genuine small-town living surrounded by farmland and rural countryside. The town is anchored by its compact historic downtown, the South Decatur Schools campus that gives the area its own school district identity, and the surrounding working farms and country lanes. Westport Park, a local recreation area with a lake, walking trails, and family-friendly amenities, gives the small community a meaningful local recreation anchor. For buyers seeking real small-town living, accessible prices, and a school district with its own community identity, Westport is one of Decatur County's most distinctive options. Living in Westport, Indiana

Life in Westport is genuinely rural and grounded in small-town character. The downtown is small but functional, with locally owned businesses, restored brick buildings, and the kind of friendly small-town atmosphere where neighbors know each other. The surrounding countryside provides quiet country roads, working farms, and the rural Hoosier landscape that has become harder to find closer to Indianapolis. Westport Park with its small lake provides a meaningful community recreation hub. The annual community calendar centers around Friday-night South Decatur Cougars athletics, summer festivals, and the steady rhythm of small-town life. For weekend recreation, residents have easy access to the Brookville Lake recreational area and broader southeastern Indiana outdoor recreation. For buyers who want a quiet small-town setting at attainable prices with southern Decatur County's rural feel, Westport delivers one of the most accessible packages in central-eastern Indiana.

Westport Home Prices & Real Estate Market

The Westport real estate market is small and personal, offering strong value within Decatur County. Homes typically range from the $150s for established single-family homes and starter properties to the $280s and beyond for larger homes, updated properties, and rural homes with substantial acreage. The market is small and inventory turns over slowly, with many transactions involving unique properties such as historic homes, working farms, hobby farms, or rural homes with substantial land. For first-time buyers, retirees, remote workers, and anyone willing to trade longer commute time for substantially lower housing costs and real country living, Westport represents some of Decatur County's strongest value. For investors, the market is thin so resale liquidity is a consideration — but well-located properties continue to attract steady tenant demand from families tied to the school district.

Schools in Westport, Indiana

Westport is served by the South Decatur Schools, which operates South Decatur Junior-Senior High School and feeder elementary schools. The district is well regarded for its small class sizes, strong community involvement, and competitive athletic programs. Families relocating to southern Decatur County who want a small-school experience often land on South Decatur Schools for exactly that reason. A Your Realty Link agent can help you confirm attendance details for any home you're considering.

Commute from Westport to Indianapolis

Westport's commute math is the longest of Decatur County's incorporated communities but workable for many buyers. Downtown Indianapolis is approximately 55 miles northwest — about a 70-minute drive via SR-3 north to I-74 west, depending on traffic. Greensburg — Decatur County's seat — sits about 15 minutes north via SR-3. Columbus, IN sits about 20 minutes west via SR-46. For remote workers, hybrid workers, retirees, and anyone willing to accommodate a longer commute in exchange for genuine country living and substantially lower housing costs, Westport offers a meaningful trade-off worth considering.
