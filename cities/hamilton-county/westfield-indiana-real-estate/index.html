Westfield Indiana Real Estate & Homes for Sale

Home of Grand Park — the largest youth sports complex in the United States — Westfield is one of Hamilton County's fastest-growing and most family-forward communities. Your Realty Link's our agents are ready to help.

📍 Hamilton County 🏫 Westfield-Washington Schools 🏡 $300s – $550s 🏟 Home of Grand Park

Westfield, Indiana has become one of the most talked-about communities in Central Indiana — and for good reason. Located in the northwest corner of Hamilton County about 30 miles north of downtown Indianapolis, Westfield has grown from a small town of a few thousand to a city of roughly 55,000 in a remarkably short time. The catalyst for much of that growth is Grand Park Sports Campus, a 400-acre youth sports complex that draws hundreds of thousands of visitors annually and put Westfield on the national map. But Grand Park is only part of the story. Families are choosing Westfield for its highly regarded Westfield-Washington Schools, its rapidly expanding restaurant and retail scene, and a growing inventory of newer homes across a range of price points. Living in Westfield, Indiana

Westfield has built its identity squarely around family and community. Grand Park Sports Campus is the most visible expression of that — each summer, the facility hosts thousands of youth tournaments across baseball, softball, soccer, and lacrosse, and serves as the official training home of the Indianapolis Colts during the NFL preseason. The energy that Grand Park brings to Westfield is palpable, and it has driven a wave of restaurants, hotels, and retail development that continues along the US-31 corridor. Downtown Westfield has also seen deliberate revitalization along Union Street, with a mix of locally owned dining spots, boutiques, and community gathering spaces that give the city a center of gravity beyond the sports campus. Neighborhood parks, youth sports leagues, and a strong calendar of community events make Westfield a place where it's easy to feel connected quickly after moving in.

Westfield Home Prices & Real Estate Market

Westfield's real estate inventory is dominated by newer construction, which reflects just how much the city has grown over the past decade. Buyers will find a wide selection of two-story and ranch-style homes in newly developed communities, with prices generally ranging from the $300s to the mid-$550s. Entry-level options in established west-side neighborhoods offer strong value for buyers who want Hamilton County schools and community amenities without the higher price tags of Carmel or Zionsville. Larger custom homes and those in communities with premium amenities push toward the upper end of the range. Because so much of Westfield's housing stock is relatively new, buyers benefit from energy-efficient construction and modern floor plans. The pace of new development also means that buyers have more choices — and sometimes more negotiating room with builders — than in more established markets nearby.

Schools in Westfield, Indiana

Westfield is served by Westfield-Washington Schools, a district that has grown alongside the city and earned a strong reputation for academic achievement and community engagement. The district operates multiple elementary schools that feed into Westfield Middle School and Westfield High School. WHS is particularly well known for its competitive athletic programs — a natural fit for a city that has made youth sports a central part of its identity — alongside solid academic offerings and a range of extracurricular options. The district's growth has been matched by investment in new facilities, ensuring that students have the resources to match their ambitions.

Commute from Westfield to Indianapolis

Westfield commuters primarily rely on the US-31 corridor, which connects directly south through Carmel and into the Indianapolis metro. Under normal traffic conditions, the drive from Westfield to downtown Indianapolis takes approximately 30 minutes. The commute to Carmel — home to major employers along the US-31 tech and healthcare corridor — is closer to 15 to 20 minutes, making Westfield a practical choice for professionals working on the north side of the metro. SR-32 provides east-west access connecting Westfield to Noblesville and communities to the west.

What is Grand Park in Westfield Indiana?

Grand Park Sports Campus is a 400-acre youth sports complex — the largest in the United States — located in Westfield. It features baseball and softball diamonds, soccer fields, and multi-use event facilities that host hundreds of thousands of visitors each year. Grand Park also serves as the official training site for the Indianapolis Colts during the NFL preseason, and has been the primary economic engine behind Westfield's remarkable growth over the past decade.

What school district serves Westfield Indiana?

Westfield is served by Westfield-Washington Schools, a growing district recognized for strong academics and competitive athletics. The district includes multiple elementary schools, Westfield Middle School, and Westfield High School. New school facilities have kept pace with the city's population growth, giving students modern learning environments.
