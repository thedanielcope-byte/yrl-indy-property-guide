Southport Indiana Real Estate & Homes for Sale

Buying or selling in Southport? Your Realty Link's our agents know the south Indianapolis market inside and out — from affordable starter homes to investment properties.

📍 Marion County 🏫 Perry Township Schools 🏡 $160s–$320s ~15 min to Downtown Indy

Southport, Indiana is a small incorporated city tucked into the southern edge of Marion County, sitting directly between Indianapolis proper and the Johnson County boundary with Greenwood. Despite its compact size — the city itself has a population of roughly 2,000 — the broader Southport neighborhood area on Indianapolis's south side is a well-established residential community with a loyal base of homeowners and strong community character. Southport Road, which runs directly through the area, connects residents to Greenwood's expansive retail corridor to the south and downtown Indianapolis to the north via I-65 in approximately 15 minutes. For buyers seeking south-side value, strong highway access, and proximity to Greenwood's amenities without paying Greenwood prices, Southport is a smart and often overlooked choice. Living in Southport, Indiana

The Southport area offers a comfortable south-side lifestyle anchored by established neighborhoods, good neighbors, and easy access to everything the southern Indianapolis metro has to offer. Residents are minutes from Greenwood Park Mall, the US-31 restaurant and retail corridor, and the many services concentrated in the Greenwood commercial district. The community has a quiet, residential character that appeals to families, retirees, and first-time buyers who want a home base that is practical rather than flashy. Local parks serve the neighborhood, and the south-side community culture — unpretentious, hardworking, and neighborly — makes Southport feel like a place where people genuinely know each other. Southport Home Prices & Real Estate Market

The Southport area delivers genuine affordability in a well-connected south-side location. Homes typically range from the $160s for smaller ranch-style and older homes in established blocks to the upper $320s for updated, larger properties with more modern amenities. The housing stock reflects the community's established age — many homes were built in the mid-20th century and offer solid construction with character, though some require updating. This makes the area particularly appealing to investors and buyers who are comfortable with cosmetic improvements in exchange for strong price-per-square-foot value. The rental market is healthy, supported by proximity to employment centers and the area's persistent affordability relative to surrounding communities.

Schools in Southport, Indiana

The Southport area is primarily served by Perry Township Schools, one of Indianapolis's larger township school districts. Perry Meridian High School serves a significant portion of the south-side community and has a strong tradition in both academics and athletics. The district operates multiple elementary and middle school campuses serving the Perry Township area of Marion County. Families considering the Southport area should confirm their specific school assignment based on their home address, as boundaries can vary within the broader south-side area.

Commute from Southport to Indianapolis

Southport's location directly on I-65 makes it one of the most commuter-convenient communities on the south side. Downtown Indianapolis is approximately 15 minutes north via I-65, and I-465 access connects residents to the metro ring road quickly. The short drive south to Greenwood — roughly 5 minutes — means Southport residents enjoy Greenwood's full retail and dining amenities while paying Marion County home prices. For those who work downtown or on the south side, few locations offer better highway convenience.
