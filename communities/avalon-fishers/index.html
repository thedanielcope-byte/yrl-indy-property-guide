Avalon of Fishers Homes for Sale

An established, family-friendly community in Fishers, convenient to Geist and I-69 and served by top Hamilton Southeastern Schools. Your Realty Link helps buyers and sellers in Avalon.

📍 Fishers · Hamilton County 🏡 $300s – $500s 🏫 Hamilton Southeastern 🚗 Near I-69 & Geist

Avalon of Fishers is an established residential community in one of central Indiana's most popular suburbs. Known for its family-friendly feel, convenient location near Geist and the I-69 corridor, and access to the highly regarded Hamilton Southeastern school system, Avalon has long been a dependable choice for buyers relocating to Fishers. Your Realty Link — an Indianapolis-based MIBOR brokerage led by Daniel Cope — helps buyers and sellers throughout Avalon and the surrounding Fishers area.

Living in Avalon of Fishers

Avalon offers the kind of settled, family-oriented environment that has made Fishers a national favorite. Streets are lined with well-kept homes and mature landscaping, and residents enjoy the community amenities and green space typical of the area's better neighborhoods. The location is a big part of the appeal: everyday shopping, restaurants, and services are close, Geist Reservoir recreation is a short drive, and the broader city of Fishers surrounds Avalon with parks, employment, and top-rated public services. It is a community that suits families, professionals, and anyone wanting an established Fishers address.

Avalon Homes &amp; Real Estate Market

Homes in Avalon generally range from the $300s to the $500s, reflecting a mix of well-maintained two-stories and family homes typical of established Fishers neighborhoods. Demand across Hamilton County is consistently strong, and Avalon's combination of location and schools keeps well-presented homes moving. Pricing depends on the specific home, size, and updates, so a local agent's perspective on value is helpful whether you are buying or selling. For buyers who want a proven Fishers community without the premium of the newest luxury developments, Avalon offers solid value.

Schools &amp; Location

Avalon is served by Hamilton Southeastern Schools, one of the most sought-after districts in Indiana and a leading reason families choose Fishers. Attendance boundaries can change, so we recommend confirming the assigned schools for a specific address before buying. Avalon's location near I-69 gives residents quick access to the Fishers employment corridor and downtown Indianapolis, generally around 30 minutes away, along with the shopping and dining around Geist.

Amenities &amp; Lifestyle

Avalon offers the neighborhood amenities and green space that Fishers buyers expect, within easy reach of the recreation, retail, and dining that define the area. Geist Reservoir's boating and waterfront options are close by, and Fishers' extensive parks and trail system add to the outdoor lifestyle. For families who value an established, well-located community with strong schools, Avalon checks the important boxes.
