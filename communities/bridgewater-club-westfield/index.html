The Bridgewater Club — Westfield Homes for Sale

A championship golf community in Westfield, Indiana, with exclusive member facilities and immaculate landscaping. Your Realty Link helps buyers and sellers throughout The Bridgewater Club and Westfield.

📍 Westfield · Hamilton County 🏡 $500s – $2M ⛳ Championship Golf & Member Club 🏫 Westfield Washington Schools

The Bridgewater Club is one of Westfield's signature golf communities — a championship golf setting with exclusive member facilities and immaculate landscaping that has long defined upscale living in the area. It is a community designed around the club experience, where the course, the member facilities, and the meticulously maintained grounds set the tone for daily life. Homes here are custom and semi-custom builds set within a polished, master-planned landscape, and the neighborhood sits inside the well-regarded Westfield Washington Schools district. Located in the heart of northern Hamilton County, The Bridgewater Club pairs a resort-quality golf lifestyle with the convenience of one of Indiana's fastest-growing suburbs. Living in The Bridgewater Club

Life in The Bridgewater Club centers on the club and course. Residents enjoy access to exclusive member facilities and a community shaped by championship golf, with immaculate landscaping that gives the neighborhood a consistently polished, well-tended appearance. The atmosphere is upscale and social — a place where golf, dining, and club events can anchor the calendar and where the grounds themselves are part of the appeal. Custom and semi-custom homes are set among mature landscaping and open, manicured spaces, creating an environment that feels both private and connected. For buyers who want a golf-first lifestyle with member amenities close to home, The Bridgewater Club delivers a well-established version of that experience. It has remained one of Westfield's most recognizable upscale communities precisely because the club and grounds are maintained to such a high standard year after year.

The Bridgewater Club Homes & Real Estate Market

The Bridgewater Club's housing is oriented toward the upper end of the Westfield market — largely custom and semi-custom homes, many positioned near the golf course or overlooking the community's landscaped spaces. Pricing generally ranges from the $500s to around $2 million, with lot, homesite location, home size, and finish level determining where a given property falls. Because homes are individually designed rather than mass-produced, there is real variety in architecture and scale, and premium homesites near the course typically command more. Demand for established luxury communities in Hamilton County remains strong, which supports values for well-maintained homes here. Whether you are buying an existing home or preparing to sell, an agent who understands the community's price tiers and the wider Westfield luxury market is key to reading value accurately.

Schools & Location

The Bridgewater Club is served by Westfield Washington Schools, a well-regarded district that has expanded with Westfield's growth and is an important draw for families. The district serves students from elementary through the large Westfield High School. Because attendance boundaries can change as the community grows, we recommend confirming the current assigned schools for any specific address directly with the district before you buy. On location, The Bridgewater Club sits in northern Hamilton County with easy access to US-31 and SR-32, connecting residents to Westfield's amenities, the broader Hamilton County corridor, and downtown Indianapolis to the south. A Your Realty Link agent can help you weigh schools, commute, and lifestyle fit as you compare homes in the community.

Amenities & Lifestyle

The defining amenity of The Bridgewater Club is its championship golf and exclusive member facilities. The club is the community's centerpiece, and the immaculate landscaping throughout reinforces the upscale, resort-like character residents value. This combination of championship golf, member-only facilities, and meticulously maintained grounds is the heart of the Bridgewater lifestyle and a primary reason luxury buyers are drawn to the community. Because membership tiers and amenity access can vary from one household to another, we recommend confirming the current club membership options, associated fees, and any homeowners-association details directly with the club and community during your purchase, so you understand exactly what is included with a given home before you commit.
