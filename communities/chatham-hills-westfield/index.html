Chatham Hills — Westfield Homes for Sale

A luxury golf community in Westfield, Indiana — championship golf, an upscale club, and resort-style amenities set among custom estate homes. Your Realty Link helps buyers and sellers throughout Chatham Hills and Westfield.

📍 Westfield · Hamilton County 🏡 $500s – $2M+ ⛳ Championship Golf & Club 🏫 Westfield Washington Schools

Chatham Hills is one of the premier luxury addresses in Westfield, a golf community built around championship golf and an upscale club with resort-style amenities. It represents the higher end of the fast-growing Westfield market — a place designed for buyers who want custom estate homes, an active club lifestyle, and a polished, master-planned setting all in one community. The neighborhood pairs its golf-centered layout with the kind of amenities more often associated with a private resort, and it sits within the well-regarded Westfield Washington Schools district. Positioned in northern Hamilton County, Chatham Hills offers a distinctive combination of luxury, recreation, and convenience. Living in Chatham Hills

Living in Chatham Hills is defined by its resort-style approach to everyday life. The community is anchored by an upscale club, and daily routines can revolve around golf, dining, and the amenities that come with a full-service club setting. Custom estate homes are set within a master-planned landscape where the golf course, water features, and open space shape the views. It attracts buyers who want more than a house — they want a lifestyle with recreation and social connection built in. The overall feel is refined and active: this is a community where residents can play a round in the morning, use the club amenities in the afternoon, and enjoy a polished, coordinated neighborhood the rest of the time. For those seeking luxury living in one of Central Indiana's fastest-growing suburbs, Chatham Hills stands among the top choices.

Chatham Hills Homes & Real Estate Market

Chatham Hills is a custom-home community, and its market reflects that. Homes are largely luxury custom builds — estate-scale residences with high-end finishes, many positioned to take advantage of golf-course or open-space views. Pricing generally starts in the $500s and climbs well beyond $2 million for the largest custom estates, with lot selection, homesite location, and the scope of each build driving where a home lands in that range. Because homes are individually built rather than mass-produced, there is meaningful variety in size, style, and price. Buyers should expect a market where premium homesites and finish levels command significant premiums. Whether you are purchasing a completed estate or selling a luxury home, working with an agent who understands the community's price tiers and the broader Westfield luxury market is essential to gauging value accurately.

Schools & Location

Chatham Hills is served by Westfield Washington Schools, a well-regarded district that has grown alongside Westfield itself and is a meaningful draw for families considering the community. The district serves students from elementary through the large Westfield High School. Because attendance boundaries can change as the area grows, we recommend confirming the current assigned schools for any specific address directly with the district before you buy. On location, Chatham Hills sits in northern Hamilton County with convenient access to US-31 and SR-32, connecting residents to Westfield's amenities, the wider Hamilton County corridor, and downtown Indianapolis to the south. A Your Realty Link agent can help you weigh schools, commute, and community fit for each home you consider.

Amenities & Lifestyle

Amenities are central to what makes Chatham Hills distinctive. The community is built around championship golf and an upscale club offering resort-style amenities, giving residents a country-club lifestyle within their own neighborhood. The club and course anchor the social and recreational life of the community, and the master-planned setting emphasizes polished landscaping, water features, and open space. This blend of golf, club amenities, and refined design is the heart of the Chatham Hills lifestyle and a major reason luxury buyers are drawn here. Because membership levels and amenity access can vary, we recommend confirming the current club membership options, any associated fees, and homeowners-association details directly with the club and community during your purchase so you know exactly what is included with a given home.
