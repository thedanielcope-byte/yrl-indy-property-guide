Irishman's Run — Zionsville Homes for Sale

Irishman's Run is a private enclave in Zionsville prized for its privacy and space — expansive estate homes set on large wooded lots that offer a secluded, natural setting while remaining minutes from the village. For buyers who want room to breathe without sacrificing Zionsville's schools and charm, Irishman's Run is a distinctive option in Boone County. Living in Irishman's Run

Irishman's Run offers a rare combination in the Indianapolis metro: genuine seclusion within easy reach of town. Homes sit on large, wooded homesites that provide privacy, mature trees, and a sense of retreat, yet Zionsville's brick Main Street, boutiques, and restaurants are only minutes away. The community appeals to buyers who value space and natural surroundings — room for larger homes, outbuildings, and outdoor living — while still wanting the schools, services, and prestige that come with a Zionsville address. It is a quieter, estate-style alternative to the more amenity-driven golf communities nearby.

Irishman's Run Homes &amp; Real Estate Market

Homes in Irishman's Run generally range from the $600s to around $1.5 million, reflecting the community's estate character — larger custom homes on substantial wooded lots. Inventory is limited by the nature of the community, which supports strong values for well-maintained properties. Because each home and lot is distinctive, pricing varies considerably with size, finish, and acreage, and the luxury and estate market behaves differently than the broader market. An agent experienced in high-end Boone County transactions can help you evaluate value accurately, whether buying or selling.

Schools &amp; Location

Irishman's Run is served by Zionsville Community Schools, consistently one of the most sought-after districts in Indiana. Attendance boundaries can change, so we recommend confirming the assigned schools for a specific address before buying. Despite its secluded feel, the community keeps residents close to Zionsville village and the main corridors toward I-65 and the broader metro, with downtown Indianapolis generally around 30 minutes away.

Amenities &amp; Lifestyle

The defining amenity at Irishman's Run is the land itself — large wooded lots that give homeowners privacy, space, and a connection to nature uncommon this close to town. Rather than a club-and-course model, the community's appeal is estate living: room for expansive homes, outdoor spaces, and a quiet setting. For buyers who prize seclusion and acreage while keeping Zionsville schools and conveniences within reach, Irishman's Run offers a lifestyle few communities in the area can match.
