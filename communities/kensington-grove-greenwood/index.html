Kensington Grove — Greenwood Homes for Sale

A Center Grove community in Greenwood with a landmark entrance, one- and two-story homes on generous lots, and a five-acre neighborhood park. Your Realty Link helps buyers and sellers in Kensington Grove.

📍 Greenwood · Johnson County 🏫 Center Grove Schools 🏡 $300s – $600s 🌳 5-Acre Neighborhood Park

Kensington Grove is a well-regarded community in the Center Grove area of Greenwood, known for its landmark roundabout and water feature at the entrance, its mix of one- and two-story homes on generous lots, and a five-acre park within the neighborhood. Combined with the sought-after Center Grove schools, these features make Kensington Grove a favorite among families on the south side. Your Realty Link — an Indianapolis-based MIBOR brokerage led by Daniel Cope — helps buyers and sellers throughout the community and Johnson County.

Living in Kensington Grove

Kensington Grove offers the kind of settled, family-friendly environment that draws buyers to the Center Grove area. A landmark roundabout and water feature mark the entrance, and inside, generous lots — ranging from roughly a third of an acre up to about an acre — give homes room to breathe. A five-acre neighborhood park provides green space and gathering room for residents. The community sits within easy reach of Greenwood's retail, dining, and services, giving families a spacious, amenity-supported neighborhood in one of the south side's most desirable school areas.

Kensington Grove Homes &amp; Real Estate Market

Homes in Kensington Grove generally range from the $300s to the $600s, reflecting a mix of one- and two-story floor plans on larger-than-average lots. The combination of Center Grove schools, lot sizes, and neighborhood amenities keeps demand steady, and well-presented homes tend to attract families relocating to the area. Pricing depends on the specific home, floor plan, and lot, so a local agent's read on value helps whether you are buying or selling. For buyers who want space and a top school area on the south side, Kensington Grove is a strong option within Johnson County.

Schools &amp; Location

Kensington Grove is served by the Center Grove school system, one of the most sought-after districts on the south side and a primary reason families choose this part of Greenwood. Attendance boundaries can change, so we recommend confirming the assigned schools for a specific address before buying. The community's location gives residents convenient access to Greenwood's retail corridors and I-65, with downtown Indianapolis generally around 25 to 30 minutes away.

Amenities &amp; Lifestyle

Kensington Grove's amenities center on space and community — the signature entrance with its roundabout and water feature, larger homesites, and a five-acre park that serves as a neighborhood gathering place. The setting suits families who want room to spread out and green space close to home, all within the Center Grove area's strong schools and Greenwood's everyday conveniences. It is a neighborhood built for settled, family-oriented living.
