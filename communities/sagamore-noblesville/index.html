Sagamore — Noblesville Homes for Sale

A custom-home golf community in Noblesville featuring a Jack Nicklaus Signature Golf Course, with homesites from villa lots to near-acre estates. Your Realty Link helps buyers and sellers in Sagamore.

📍 Noblesville · Hamilton County ⛳ Jack Nicklaus Signature 🏡 $400s – $1M+ 🏫 Noblesville Schools

Sagamore is one of Hamilton County's premier golf communities — a custom-home development in Noblesville built around a Jack Nicklaus Signature Golf Course. With homes by the area's respected builders and homesites ranging from villa lots to near-acre estates, Sagamore offers golf-course living at a range of price points within one prestigious community. Your Realty Link — an Indianapolis-based MIBOR brokerage led by Daniel Cope — helps buyers and sellers throughout Sagamore and the Noblesville area.

Living in Sagamore

Sagamore is built for buyers who want golf-course living with room to choose their scale. The community's Jack Nicklaus Signature course anchors the setting, and homesites range from lower-maintenance villa lots to generously sized estate parcels approaching an acre — with golf, wooded, water-view, and walkout options. That variety means Sagamore accommodates downsizers, move-up families, and buyers building a custom dream home alike. Set in Noblesville, residents enjoy the community's amenities while staying close to Morse Reservoir, the historic downtown square, and the shopping and employment of northern Hamilton County.

Sagamore Homes &amp; Real Estate Market

Homes in Sagamore generally range from the $400s to $1 million and above, a spread that reflects the community's variety of lot types and home sizes — from villa-style residences to large custom estates on premium golf and water-view sites. Demand for Hamilton County golf communities is strong, and Sagamore's Nicklaus pedigree and custom-home quality support solid values. Because homes and homesites vary widely, pricing depends heavily on lot type, size, and finish. An agent who knows the community can help you evaluate value accurately across Hamilton County.

Schools &amp; Location

Sagamore is served by Noblesville Schools, a sought-after Hamilton County district and a key draw for families. Attendance boundaries can change, so we recommend confirming the assigned schools for a specific address before buying. Sagamore's location in Noblesville keeps residents near Morse Reservoir and the SR-37/SR-32 corridors, with the Fishers and Carmel employment centers a reasonable drive and downtown Indianapolis generally around 35 to 40 minutes away.

Amenities &amp; Lifestyle

The heart of Sagamore is its Jack Nicklaus Signature Golf Course — a marquee design that gives the community genuine golf credibility — surrounded by custom homes on varied, well-planned homesites. Many homes enjoy course, wooded, or water views, and the range of lot types supports a lifestyle from low-maintenance villa living to expansive estate ownership. For buyers who want signature golf and custom-home quality in a Noblesville setting, Sagamore is among the area's standout communities.
