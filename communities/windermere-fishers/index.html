Windermere — Fishers Homes for Sale

An established golf-course community in Fishers near Geist Reservoir, built around Ironwood Golf Course, with everything from low-maintenance condos to large custom homes on the course. Your Realty Link helps buyers and sellers in Windermere.

📍 Fishers · Hamilton County ⛳ Ironwood Golf Course 🏡 $300s – $700s+ 🏫 Hamilton Southeastern

Windermere is one of Fishers' established golf-course communities, set near Geist Reservoir and built around the Ironwood Golf Course. It is known for its variety: the community encompasses a range of subdivisions, offering everything from low-maintenance condos to spacious custom homes backing up to the course. That mix, plus mature landscaping and resort-style amenities, has made Windermere a durable favorite in a city known for strong schools and steady demand. Your Realty Link — an Indianapolis-based MIBOR brokerage led by Daniel Cope — helps buyers and sellers throughout Windermere and the Fishers area.

Living in Windermere

Life in Windermere centers on the golf course, the water, and the community's amenities. Mature, tree-lined streets wind past a range of home styles, and residents enjoy access to a clubhouse, swimming pool, tennis courts, walking trails, and plenty of green space. The community's location near Geist Reservoir puts waterfront recreation, shopping, and restaurants within a short drive, while the broader city of Fishers — consistently ranked among the best places to live in the country — surrounds it with parks, employment, and top-rated services. It is a setting that appeals to golfers, families, and buyers who want established amenities in a well-run community.

Windermere Homes &amp; Real Estate Market

Windermere's housing stock is unusually varied for a single community, which gives buyers a wide range of entry points. Low-maintenance condos and smaller homes generally start in the $300s, while larger two-stories and custom homes — particularly those on or near the golf course — reach into the $500s, $600s, and above. That range means Windermere attracts everyone from downsizers and first-time Fishers buyers to move-up families wanting a course-side home. Demand across Hamilton County keeps well-presented homes here active. Prices vary with the specific subdivision, home size, and golf-course frontage, so a local agent's read on value is worthwhile.

Schools &amp; Location

Windermere is served by Hamilton Southeastern Schools, one of the most sought-after districts in the state and a major reason families choose Fishers. As with any community, attendance boundaries can change, so we recommend confirming the assigned schools for a specific address before buying. Windermere's location near Geist and the I-69 corridor gives residents quick access to Fishers' employment and retail, with downtown Indianapolis generally around 30 minutes away depending on traffic.

Amenities &amp; Lifestyle

The Ironwood Golf Course anchors Windermere, and many homes enjoy course or water views. Community amenities include a clubhouse, swimming pool, tennis courts, ponds, and walking trails, giving residents a resort-style lifestyle without leaving the neighborhood. Proximity to Geist Reservoir adds boating, waterfront dining, and additional recreation just minutes away. For buyers who want an amenity-rich, golf-oriented community with the schools and services of Fishers, Windermere delivers a well-rounded package.
