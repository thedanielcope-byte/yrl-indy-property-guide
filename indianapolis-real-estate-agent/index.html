Why Your Agent Choice Matters

In a market as diverse as Indianapolis — where prices, inventory, and competition can vary dramatically from one zip code to the next — experience and local knowledge matter enormously. A great agent knows not just how to fill out a contract, but how to price a home strategically, negotiate terms that protect your interests, and navigate the unexpected twists that happen in almost every real estate transaction.

The Indianapolis metro is served by hundreds of agents, but not all are equally equipped. MIBOR membership means access to the full MLS — every active listing, recent comparable sale, and market trend data. It also means professional standards and accountability. How to Choose the Right Indianapolis Agent

If you're interviewing agents, here are the questions worth asking:

  • Are you a MIBOR member with full MLS access?
  • How many transactions have you closed in the specific area I'm buying or selling in?
  • What does your marketing plan look like for my home?
  • How do you communicate with clients — and how quickly do you respond?
  • Can you walk me through the current buyer compensation agreement?
  • What happens if something goes sideways during the transaction?

Your Realty Link welcomes these conversations. We believe clients deserve straight answers before they sign anything.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right real estate agent in Indianapolis?

Look for a licensed MIBOR member who knows your target neighborhood, communicates clearly, and has a track record of successful transactions. Ask how they'll market your home or find you listings — and pay attention to whether they give you straight answers or just tell you what you want to hear. Your Realty Link offers free consultations with no pressure.

Does it cost money to work with a buyer's agent in Indianapolis?

In most transactions, buyer representation costs the buyer nothing out of pocket. Agent compensation is negotiated as part of the transaction. Your Realty Link will walk you through the current buyer compensation agreement process clearly before you begin touring homes.

What areas does Your Realty Link serve?

Your Realty Link serves buyers and sellers across Central Indiana, including Marion, Hamilton, Johnson, Hendricks, Boone, Hancock, Shelby, Morgan, and Madison counties — and additional counties beyond. If you're buying or selling in Central Indiana, contact YRL to confirm coverage in your area.
