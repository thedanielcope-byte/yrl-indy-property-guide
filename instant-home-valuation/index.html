What Affects Your Home's Value in Indianapolis?

Location & Neighborhood

School district, proximity to employment corridors, walkability, and neighborhood trajectory all drive value in Central Indiana.

Recent Comparable Sales

What homes like yours have sold for in the last 3-6 months is the single strongest indicator of your home's current market value.

Home Size & Condition

Square footage, bedroom/bath count, lot size, age, and overall condition are factored into every valuation — automated or agent-prepared.

Updates & Upgrades

New kitchen, bathrooms, roof, HVAC, and curb appeal improvements add real value — but automated tools may undercount them. A CMA captures this better.
