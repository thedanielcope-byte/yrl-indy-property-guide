New Construction Homes in Indianapolis & Central Indiana

Central Indiana is one of the most active new construction markets in the Midwest. Before you walk into a builder's model home alone, know this: the sales agent works for the builder — not you. Your Realty Link represents new construction buyers at no extra cost.

New construction is booming across Central Indiana — from large master-planned communities in Westfield and McCordsville to boutique infill builds closer to Indianapolis. It's an exciting option for buyers who want modern finishes, energy efficiency, and a home that hasn't been lived in before. But buying new construction is not as simple as picking your countertops — and going in without representation can cost you. Why You Need Your Own Agent for New Construction

This is the most important thing to understand about buying a new build: the agent in the builder's model home represents the builder. Their job is to protect the builder's interests — not yours. That means the contract they hand you is drafted in the builder's favor. Upgrade pricing, lot premiums, closing cost contributions, inspection rights, and warranty terms all have room for negotiation — but only if you have someone advocating for your side.

A Your Realty Link buyer's agent reviews builder contracts before you sign, identifies terms that deserve negotiation, helps you understand what's included versus what costs extra, and attends walkthroughs and pre-closing inspections with you. All of this at no cost above what the builder has already built into the transaction.

Important: To have buyer representation on a new construction purchase, you typically need to bring your agent with you on your first visit to the model home or register them before your first contact with the builder. Contact Your Realty Link before you visit any model homes so you're protected from day one.

New Construction Price Ranges in Central Indiana

New construction prices vary significantly by location, builder, and spec level. Here's a general sense of what buyers can expect in key markets:

  • Hamilton County (Westfield, Noblesville, Fishers): Typically $350s–$700s+ depending on community and spec level
  • Hendricks County (Avon, Brownsburg, Plainfield): Generally $280s–$500s
  • Boone County (Whitestown, Zionsville area): $300s–$600s+
  • Johnson County (Greenwood, Bargersville): $270s–$450s
  • Hancock County (McCordsville, Greenfield): $290s–$500s

Frequently Asked Questions — New Construction

Do I need my own agent to buy new construction in Indianapolis?

Yes — and it costs you nothing extra. The builder's sales agent represents the builder, not you. Your Realty Link provides independent buyer representation throughout the new construction process, reviewing contracts, negotiating upgrades and incentives, and protecting your interests from contract signing through closing.

Where is new construction happening in Central Indiana?

Active new construction communities are concentrated in Hamilton County (Westfield, Noblesville, Fishers), Hendricks County (Avon, Brownsburg, Plainfield), Boone County (Whitestown, Zionsville area), Johnson County (Bargersville, Greenwood), and Hancock County (McCordsville, Greenfield). Your Realty Link can show you active communities in any of these areas.

Can I negotiate the price on a new construction home?

Builders rarely reduce base price significantly, but there is often meaningful room to negotiate on upgrades, closing cost contributions, lot premiums, and included options.
