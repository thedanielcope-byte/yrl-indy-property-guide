Privacy Policy

How Indy Property Guide and Your Realty Link collect, use, and protect your information.

Effective Date: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Indy Property Guide (indypropertyguide.com), operated by Your Realty Link, collects, uses, and protects information from visitors to this website. Your Realty Link is a licensed Indiana real estate brokerage located at 2302 E Southport Rd, Indianapolis, IN 46227.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you use our website, including:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, and phone number when you submit a form on our site.
  • Inquiry details: Your message content, interest type (buying, selling, investing, etc.), and the page you submitted the form from.
  • Usage data: We may collect non-personal information such as browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent on our site through standard analytics tools.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as Social Security numbers, financial account numbers, or government-issued identification through this website.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used to:

  • Respond to your real estate inquiry or request
  • Connect you with a licensed Your Realty Link agent who can assist you
  • Provide you with a home valuation, property search assistance, or other requested service
  • Send follow-up communications related to your inquiry
  • Improve our website content and user experience

We will not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to unrelated third parties.

How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Your Realty Link agents: Licensed agents within our brokerage who can assist with your real estate needs.
  • Service providers: Third-party tools we use to manage leads and communications, such as our customer relationship management (CRM) system. These providers are contractually required to protect your data.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation.

Cookies and Tracking

This website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) that collect anonymous usage data to help us understand how visitors interact with our site. This data does not personally identify you.

Third-Party Links

This site contains links to third-party websites, including yourrealtylink.com (our main brokerage site), MLS property search tools, and social media platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party site you visit.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you provide through our website. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information from our systems
  • Opt out of follow-up communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at csirealtyteam@yourrealtylink.com or call 317-201-6323.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
