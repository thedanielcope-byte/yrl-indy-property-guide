Perry Township Schools Homes for Sale

On Indianapolis's south side, the MSD of Perry Township offers established neighborhoods and affordability inside Marion County. Your Realty Link helps buyers and sellers throughout the district.

📍 South Marion County 🏫 Perry Township Schools 🏡 $150s – $400s Affordable South-Side Living

On the south side of Indianapolis, the Metropolitan School District of Perry Township serves a large, established area that gives families a well-known district and some of the more attainable home prices inside Marion County. For buyers who want to stay in Indianapolis without stretching their budget, the Perry Township district is a practical and popular choice. This guide covers buying a home in the district — the area, the market, and how Your Realty Link, an Indianapolis-based MIBOR brokerage led by Daniel Cope, helps families buy and sell here.

Living in the Perry Township Area

Perry Township covers much of south-side Marion County, including the area around Southport. It is one of the most established parts of the south side, with mature neighborhoods, ranch and two-story homes from a range of eras, parks, and long-standing community institutions. The area offers everyday convenience — retail along Madison Avenue and US-31, easy interstate access, and a settled, neighborly feel — at prices that remain among the more affordable inside the county. It appeals to first-time buyers, families, and anyone who wants an Indianapolis address without a north-side price tag.

Perry Township Area Home Prices &amp; Real Estate Market

Homes in the Perry Township school district generally range from the $150s to the $400s, one of the broader and more affordable spreads inside Marion County. Established ranches and starter homes anchor the lower end, while larger or updated homes and newer pockets reach toward the top. The affordability keeps demand strong from first-time and value-focused buyers, and well-priced homes tend to move steadily. For buyers who want a recognized district and an Indianapolis location without stretching, Perry Township is one of the south side's most practical options. A Your Realty Link agent can confirm which homes fall within the district boundary.

About MSD of Perry Township

The Metropolitan School District of Perry Township serves the south side of Indianapolis with elementary, middle, and high school programs, and it is one of the reasons families choose this part of Marion County. We do not publish specific ratings, test scores, or enrollment numbers here because those change from year to year — buyers should confirm current details directly with the district. Attendance boundaries can shift over time, so verifying the assigned schools for any specific address before buying is important. A Your Realty Link agent can help you confirm boundaries for the homes you are considering.

Commute &amp; Location

Perry Township's south-side location offers strong access to I-65, I-465, and US-31, putting downtown Indianapolis generally around 15 to 20 minutes away depending on where in the township you live. The area's road network makes commutes to downtown, the airport, and the southern suburbs straightforward, and established retail corridors keep everyday needs close to home.
