The Indianapolis Home Selling Timeline

Understanding the process takes a lot of the stress out of selling. Here's a typical timeline for an Indianapolis home sale with Your Realty Link:

  1. Initial consultation & CMA (Week 1): Daniel tours your home, reviews comparable sales, and presents a realistic pricing strategy.
  2. Pre-listing preparation (Weeks 1–2): YRL advises on any improvements or staging that will improve buyer perception and help the home photograph well.
  3. Go live on MLS (Week 2–3): Your listing goes live on the MIBOR MLS and begins receiving showings.
  4. Offers & negotiation (Varies): In active markets, offers can come quickly. YRL reviews each one with you and negotiates terms that protect your interests.
  5. Under contract to closing (30–45 days typically): Inspections, appraisals, and title work all happen during this period. YRL coordinates the moving pieces so you don't have to.

Specialty Selling Situations

Not every home sale is straightforward. Your Realty Link has experience with a wide range of selling situations that require extra care and expertise:

  • Expired listings — your home didn't sell the first time, and you need a new strategy
  • Divorce home sales — coordinating a sale during a difficult life transition
  • Estate and probate sales — handling inherited or estate-owned property
  • Sell fast situations — relocation, job changes, or financial urgency
  • FSBO conversions — you tried to sell yourself and are ready for professional help
