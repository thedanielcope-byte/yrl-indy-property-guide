When a Cash Sale Makes Sense

There are legitimate situations where selling for cash is the right move. Here are the most common reasons sellers in Indianapolis come to us looking for cash buyers:

Speed Is the Priority

Job relocation, financial pressure, or a time-sensitive situation where you can't wait 45-60 days for a financed buyer to close.

Home Needs Major Repairs

Foundation issues, roof damage, outdated systems — your home may not qualify for FHA or VA financing, making cash or conventional buyers the realistic pool.

Inherited or Vacant Property

You've inherited a home you don't want to maintain, or you own a vacant property that's costing you money every month it sits.

Avoiding Financing Risk

Cash deals don't fall through because of appraisal shortfalls or buyer mortgage denials. If certainty of close matters more than maximum price, cash offers provide that.

Whatever your situation, the key is understanding what your home is actually worth before accepting any offer — and that's where having an agent on your side makes a real difference.

Frequently Asked Questions — Cash Home Buyers

Are cash home buyer companies legitimate?

Some are, and some are not. Many "we buy houses" companies are legitimate investors, but their offers are typically 50-70% of market value. Others use bait-and-switch tactics or hidden fees. Your Realty Link helps you evaluate cash offers against your home's true market value so you can make an informed decision.

How fast can I close with a cash buyer?

Cash sales can close in as little as 7-14 days because there's no mortgage approval process. A more typical timeline is 14-30 days to allow for inspections, title work, and closing preparation. Your Realty Link works with your timeline and can coordinate a fast closing when speed is the priority.

Will I get less money selling to a cash buyer?

Not necessarily. While "we buy houses" companies typically offer below market value, individual cash buyers and investor groups purchasing through the MLS often pay competitive prices. The key is marketing your home to the right pool of buyers. Your Realty Link exposes your home to both cash and financed buyers to generate the strongest possible offers.

Do I need to make repairs before selling to a cash buyer?

Usually not. Cash buyers — especially investors — are often willing to purchase homes as-is, including properties that need significant repairs. Your Realty Link will advise you on whether minor improvements would meaningfully increase your sale price or whether selling in current condition is the better financial decision.
