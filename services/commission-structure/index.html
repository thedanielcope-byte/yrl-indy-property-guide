How Real Estate Commission Splits Work

When a commission is earned on a transaction, it's typically divided between the agent and the brokerage according to an agreed split. From there, brokerages differ enormously: some charge monthly desk fees, per-transaction fees, or technology fees; some have caps after which you keep more; some bundle in tools and support, while others charge à la carte. The key is to look past the headline split and understand the complete picture. Daniel Cope will walk you through exactly how our structure works — clearly and honestly — so you can compare it accurately against your current or prospective brokerage.
