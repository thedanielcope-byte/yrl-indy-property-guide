Frequently Asked Questions — Experienced Agents

Why would an experienced agent switch brokerages?

Common reasons include a better commission split, less corporate overhead, more responsive support, a stronger team culture, or simply wanting to be treated like a valued professional rather than a number. The right brokerage should make your business easier, not harder.

What does Your Realty Link offer experienced agents?

A competitive, transparent commission structure, direct access to the broker, a collaborative team culture, and the freedom to run your business without the bureaucracy of a large franchise. We support your production rather than getting in its way.

Is switching brokerages complicated?

It's more straightforward than many agents expect. We help make the transition smooth — transferring your license, your active business, and getting you set up with our tools and MLS access quickly so you don't lose momentum.

Will I keep my current clients and listings?

Generally, yes — your client relationships are yours. Active listings are handled according to your current agreements, and we'll help you navigate the transition professionally. We're happy to discuss the specifics of your situation.
