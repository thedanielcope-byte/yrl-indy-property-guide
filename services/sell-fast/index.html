What "Fast" Actually Looks Like in the Indianapolis Market

In the Central Indiana market, a well-priced home in good condition can realistically go from listing to accepted offer in one to two weeks — sometimes faster. From accepted offer to closing typically takes 30 to 45 days, depending on the buyer's financing. That means a seller who is ready to move when their listing goes live can be at the closing table in six to eight weeks from the decision to sell. Sellers who need to close even faster may want to explore cash buyer options, which we can discuss honestly — including what those offers typically look like compared to what you'd net on the open market. We'll give you real numbers so you can make an informed decision, not one based on urgency alone.

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can I realistically sell my home in Indianapolis?

A well-priced home in good condition in a desirable Central Indiana market can receive offers within the first one to two weeks and close in 30 to 45 days from an accepted offer. The timeline depends heavily on pricing accuracy, home presentation, and current market conditions in your specific area. Your Realty Link will give you a realistic expectation based on your home and neighborhood — not a promise we can't keep.

Do I have to accept a low price to sell my home fast?

No — and this is one of the biggest misconceptions about fast home sales. Speed and price are not opposites. A correctly priced, well-presented home often sells faster AND at a better price than an overpriced home that has to reduce its price multiple times. The key is strategic pricing, not discounting. Your Realty Link's goal is always to maximize your proceeds — even on a fast timeline.

Should I consider a cash offer from a home buying company?

Cash offers from iBuyers and home buying companies are fast and convenient, but they typically come in significantly below market value — sometimes 10 to 20 percent less. For sellers who have the time and the right home, selling on the open market through the MIBOR MLS with Your Realty Link almost always results in a better net. We'll help you evaluate any cash offer you receive so you can make an informed comparison.

What should I do to prepare my home for a fast sale?

Focus on first impressions: curb appeal, a clean and decluttered interior, fresh paint where needed, and making sure every light works and every surface is clean. Professional listing photos make a dramatic difference. Your Realty Link will walk through your home before listing and give you a prioritized list of what to address — we won't suggest expensive renovations that won't add value, but we will tell you what matters to buyers.
