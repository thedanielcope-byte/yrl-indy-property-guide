Buying a home later in life is a different experience than buying your first one — and it should be. Your priorities have changed. You know what you want, what you don't, and what matters most for your day-to-day comfort and peace of mind. Maybe you're looking for a single-story home with no stairs to worry about. Maybe you want to be closer to your grandchildren, your doctors, or your favorite places. Maybe you're simply ready for a home that fits the way you live now, not the way you lived twenty years ago. Whatever is bringing you to this decision, Your Realty Link understands that this isn't just a transaction — it's a deeply personal move, and we treat it that way.

What Senior Home Buyers Are Looking For

Every senior buyer has their own reasons for moving, but we see some common themes among the clients we work with across Central Indiana:

Accessibility & Comfort

Main-floor living, step-free entries, wider doorways, walk-in showers, and layouts that work now and in the years ahead.

Low Maintenance

Many seniors want less yard work and upkeep — condos, patio homes, and 55+ communities with HOA-maintained exteriors are popular choices.

Location & Proximity

Being close to healthcare providers, grocery stores, places of worship, and family members often drives the search more than square footage.

Right-Sizing, Not Downgrading

Moving to a smaller home doesn't mean settling for less. Many seniors find a newer, better-designed home that actually feels more comfortable than their current one.

These are all things that Your Realty Link actively considers when searching for homes with our senior clients. We don't just pull up listings — we think carefully about how each property fits your specific needs.

Frequently Asked Questions — Senior Home Buyers

What should senior home buyers look for in a home?

Prioritize single-story living or a main-floor master suite, step-free entries, wide doorways, walk-in showers, and low-maintenance exteriors. Proximity to healthcare, shopping, and family is also important. Your Realty Link helps you evaluate every home with long-term comfort and accessibility in mind.

Are there 55+ communities in Indianapolis?

Yes. Central Indiana has several active-adult and 55+ communities in areas like Fishers, Carmel, Greenwood, and Brownsburg. These communities typically feature maintenance-free living, community amenities, and social activities. Your Realty Link can help you compare options and find the right fit.

Can I use VA or reverse mortgage funds to buy a home?

Yes. VA loans remain available to eligible veterans at any age, and the HECM for Purchase program allows qualifying seniors aged 62 and older to buy a home with no monthly mortgage payments. Your Realty Link works with lenders experienced in both of these programs.

Does Your Realty Link help with selling and buying at the same time?

Absolutely. Many of our senior clients are selling a larger home and buying a smaller one simultaneously. Daniel Cope coordinates both transactions so your timing, finances, and move logistics all work together smoothly.
